Our history

From a small classroom to a global academy

LingoUp began in 2010 as a single classroom in downtown Chicago, founded by two language teachers who believed that adults could learn faster when lessons were built around real-life goals — not textbook exercises.

Within five years, demand grew beyond the classroom. We expanded to online delivery, hired certified native teachers from across the globe and built our proprietary fast-track curriculum. Today, LingoUp students are in 40+ countries — negotiating deals in Mandarin, presenting in English and travelling the world in Spanish.

Our focus has never changed: give adults the fastest, most practical path to real-world language fluency.

What drives us

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To give every adult learner the fastest, most practical path to real-world language fluency — so they can seize every professional and personal opportunity a new language unlocks.

Vision

A world where language is never a barrier to career growth, education or human connection — where anyone, anywhere, can communicate across cultures with confidence.

Values

Results over routine. Authentic practice over drills. Respect for the learner's time, goals and pace. And an uncompromising commitment to quality in every single class.

How we teach

Our Methodology

Total Immersion

From lesson one, classes are conducted in the target language. Immersion activates natural acquisition and builds fluency without translation dependency.

Real-World Practice

Every exercise is grounded in authentic scenarios — job interviews, business calls, airport conversations, meeting negotiations. You practice what you'll actually use.

Native Teachers

All teachers are certified native speakers. You absorb natural intonation, cultural nuance and colloquial expressions that no textbook can teach.

Progress Monitoring

Weekly feedback reports, monthly level assessments and a personal learning dashboard keep you on track and motivated towards your specific goal.
